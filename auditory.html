The Exploration of Contemporary Mediums

The modern artist has a plethora of tools available to them to help bring their vision to fruition.

This website is dedicated to the appreciation and understanding of aesthetics in the modern world.

This website caters to the needs of writers and artists that wish to enrich their imagination.

Content Organization of This Site

This website is divided into three broad hierarchies.

Images of digital art will be available in gallery format in the Visual Tab.

Videos of music that are partitioned according to their emotional nuances are available in the Auditory Tab.

Writings and recommendations can be found in the Semantic Tab.
