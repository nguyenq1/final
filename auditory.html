Anticipation

01 Anticipation

Anticipation for an ensuing conflict is the general theme of this playlist.

For example, a chase scene or the beginning of a combat scenario in which the combatants have yet to fully commit (such as a light skirmish or the issuing of an ultimatum).

Pressure, trepidation, and certain aspects of internal conflict are likely to be represented.
