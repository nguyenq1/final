Fate/Zero

Read Fate/Zero

Fate/Zero is a light novel that serves as to the prequel to the visual novel Fate/Stay Night

It was also later adapted into an animated series.

I chose Fate/Zero to recommend because it shows how a universe can be fleshed out through various different mediums.

By listening to the soundtrack composed for the "Fate" series, reading its novel, experiencing its visual novel,

and watching the animated series, one is able to experience the vision of its creators in a full auditory, visual and semantic experience.

The premise of Fate/Zero is as follows: 7 Mages called "Masters" each summon a powerful "Servant" from the past to war wage for the Holy Grail,

an omnipresent device said to grant wishes. These servants range from heroes of the past like Alexander the Great, to figures of legend like King Arthur,

to great rulers in history like Gilgamesh, king of Babylon. Only 1 Master and their Servant can claim victory and therefore the Holy Grail.

As the story nears its conclusion, the true nature of the Holy Grail is revealed.
